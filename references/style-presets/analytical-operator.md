# Analytical Operator Preset

Use this preset when the user asks for "my style", references a previously supplied writing sample, or wants a direct internal management-report voice.

## Positioning

- Voice: analytical operator
- Context: internal strategy memo, industry mode report, management review, research note
- Goal: make the report easy to scan, easy to reuse, and easy to turn into action

## Title rules

- Use explicit report titles such as `XX行业模式研究报告`
- Put time range on a separate line when needed
- Write level-1 headings as complete judgment or definition sentences
- Headings must make clear what the section is proving

## Body rules

- Start each section with a direct definition or judgment
- Long complete paragraphs are allowed
- Paragraphs may mix definition, fact, explanation, comparison, and conclusion when the logic stays clear
- Prefer bullets when information density is high
- Prefer tables for model comparison, platform comparison, and economics comparison
- Keep one core point per bullet when using bullets

## Language rules

- Use a rational, direct, management-report tone
- Favor business words such as `模式`, `链路`, `利润来源`, `行业定位`, `投放情况`, `客单价`, `抓手`, `水位`, `颗粒度`
- Keep abstractions anchored to concrete business meaning
- Use numbers, ratios, and ranges when evidence exists
- Avoid inflated certainty and avoid slogan-like phrasing

## Structural preferences

- Explain the object first
- Then split out model, chain, platform, economics, and operating implications
- Use `3.1`, `3.2`, `3.3` when a section becomes complex
- Keep the logic between heading and body explicit

## Formatting quirks

- Natural paragraphs should not end with a full-width Chinese period
- Use `●` for key facts and `○` for sub-points when bullets are helpful
- Do not force a fixed four-part essay structure if the user asked for another layout

## Hard constraints

- Do not write `不是……而是……` or close variants such as `并非……而是……` and `不是在……而是在……`
- Do not use loose essay-style transitions
- Do not hide evidence gaps with confident wording
- Do not let style imitation override factual caution

## When to relax this preset

Relax preset-specific preferences if the user explicitly asks for a different audience voice, another formatting scheme, or another sentence rhythm. Keep only the parts that still fit the request, but do not relax system-level bans from [style-system.md](../style-system.md).
